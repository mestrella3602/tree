Trees in New York

In our code we are displaying all the trees in New York and a allowing the user to enter ether a New York borough or New York zip code then click a button that connects them to google maps which shows the exact location of the tree. The trees are individually put on cards which say weather that tree has a good health, fair health or poor health. The a cards are color coded to show good, fair and poor health as well as say if the tree is alive or dead and if it has any problems.
